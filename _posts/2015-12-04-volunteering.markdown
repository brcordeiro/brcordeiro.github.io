I’m often hearing people talking about work-life, personal life and how they try to balance it. Ok, few years ago, I was in the same group. Then, something great happened into my life: I started to travel. Somehow, who I was never came back from the first trip. I couldn't see the world as the same way, I realized that the world is not so big as we usually think and each person has something in common to each other, doesn't matter the difference of culture.

You might be wondering – Ok, nice story, but how it is connected to a tech blog? Calm down, I will get there.

So, I wanted do some volunteering work giving some contribution for a better world but with my unstable schedule was hard to find something to be committed. Then, after some time, I tried to find something connecting my job, my personal life and people around the world, because in the end, is like that, there is only one thing, LIFE.

Some minutes later and some researches on google, I found some interesting projects that we can help even from home. So, no excuses, you can make difference wherever you are! Here are their links:

<a href="http://datakind.org/">DataKind</a>: Harnessing the power of data science in the service of humanity.

<a href="http://enablingthefuture.org/">Enabling the future</a>: A global network of passionate volunteers using 3D printing to give the world a "Helping Hand."
